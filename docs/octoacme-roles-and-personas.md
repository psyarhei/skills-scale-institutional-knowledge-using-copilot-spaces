# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards. 

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications.  They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers drive the user experience by creating intuitive interfaces and validating design decisions with users.  They collaborate closely with Product Managers to understand requirements and with Developers to ensure feasible implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Define user workflows and information architecture
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Document design systems and component standards
- Provide design feedback during code review and QA

### Goals
- Deliver interfaces that are intuitive and meet user needs
- Ensure consistency in design across releases
- Reduce friction and improve user satisfaction metrics

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager and Developers
- Usability testing sessions with users
- Design documentation in Figma or equivalent tools
- Feedback during QA and release planning

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads establish quality standards, manage test execution, and report on product quality. They work across teams to ensure acceptance criteria are met and establish confidence for production releases.

### Responsibilities
- Develop comprehensive test plans aligned with acceptance criteria
- Manage manual and automated testing execution
- Identify, document, and track bugs with clear reproduction steps
- Report on quality metrics and test coverage
- Coordinate go/no-go decisions with Project Manager and stakeholders

### Goals
- Ensure product meets acceptance criteria before release
- Maintain high test coverage and defect prevention
- Provide confidence in release quality

### Typical Communication
- Test planning meetings during project planning
- Daily test execution updates in standups
- Bug reports and quality metrics dashboards
- Release validation and sign-off coordination

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers maintain infrastructure, automate deployments, and ensure reliable release processes. They enable the team to deploy frequently with confidence and maintain system health in production.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Automate build, test, and deployment workflows
- Monitor infrastructure performance and reliability
- Manage configuration management and secrets
- Support release coordination and rollback procedures

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Maintain visibility into system health and performance

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure design reviews with Developers
- Release coordination with Project Manager
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Observability and alerting dashboards

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical execution by documenting requirements, mapping processes, and analyzing impact.  They help translate stakeholder goals into actionable product and technical tasks.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze and map current and future business processes
- Clarify acceptance criteria with Product Manager and Developers
- Support data analysis and impact measurement
- Help identify dependencies and cross-team implications

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between business needs and delivery
- Reduce rework due to unclear or missed requirements
- Enable informed decision-making with data analysis

### Typical Communication
- Requirement gathering sessions with business stakeholders
- Collaboration with Product Manager on backlog clarity
- Technical clarification with Developers
- Data reports and impact analysis with leadership

---

## Support / Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success teams are the voice of the customer, providing feedback on usability, bugs, and feature requests. They help triage issues post-release and support knowledge base updates.

### Responsibilities
- Collect and triage customer-reported issues
- Provide feedback to Product Manager on feature requests and pain points
- Assist in root cause analysis for production incidents
- Update and maintain customer-facing documentation
- Coordinate with QA and DevOps on issue reproduction and resolution

### Goals
- Improve customer satisfaction and reduce support load
- Provide product team with direct user insights
- Accelerate resolution of critical customer-impacting issues

### Typical Communication
- Weekly feedback summaries to Product Manager
- Incident coordination with Project Manager and DevOps
- Collaboration with QA on issue reproduction
- Knowledge base and documentation updates

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise. 
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- When planning or executing projects, refer to these roles to ensure all critical functions are represented and their responsibilities are clear.
